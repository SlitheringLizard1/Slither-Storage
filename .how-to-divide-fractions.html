Fractions

Source

This page helps you with dividing fractions.

Math can be hard, especially when working with fractions. When you get the hang of them, they are very easy to do math with.

    Instructions

First, you need to do something called Keep, Change, Flip. Keep the first side of the equation, Change the problem to multiplication, and Flip the numerator and the denominator of the second fraction.

Next, look for any cross-simplifications you can do to make the problem easier.

Then, you can just multiply across, and you have your answer. Simple as that!
